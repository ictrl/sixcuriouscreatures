Who are We!

We know we are a little different from the rest of the networking meetups you have attended. So let us explain what we do and how we do it. According to 6 degrees of separation, every person in the world is connected to each other through four other people in the middle. We are here and doing it quite literally!

Events

How do we do it?


  • You and a friend can enroll as co-hosts for an event on a set date. You guys will be call the co-hosts.

  • You and you friend (Co-hosts) can invite one person each, who you enjoy spending time with and think are interesting.

  • Then, both of your invitees are expected to invite someone that they think is interesting and send them the invitations as well.

  • If you don’t have a friend who fits the profile thats fine too! Just tell us who it is and we will put them in a different cohort.
